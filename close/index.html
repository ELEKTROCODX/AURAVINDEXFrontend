How to Request Account Deletion

If you wish to permanently close your AURA VINDEX account, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to aura24vindex@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: CLOSE ACCOUNT - AURA VINDEX.
  3. Optionally, include your reason for closing your account.

What Happens When You Close Your Account?

  • All your personal data (name, email, birth date, gender, address, profile picture) will be permanently deleted from our servers.
  • Your account will no longer be accessible.
  • For audit and internal record-keeping purposes, your unique user ID will remain in our database.

Please note that this process is irreversible.
